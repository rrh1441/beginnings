Quick Answers

When can I schedule a tour?

Tours are offered Tuesday through Thursday, typically between 9:30 AM and 11:00 AM when you can see classrooms in action. After submitting your inquiry, we'll reach out to schedule a time that works for you.

Should I inquire even if I don't need care right away?

Absolutely. Many families inquire during pregnancy or months in advance. Given waitlists, especially for infants, early inquiry is encouraged. There's no penalty for planning ahead.

Can I visit both locations?

Yes! Many families tour both before deciding. Just let us know you'd like to see both, and we'll coordinate tours at each site.

What if I have questions before touring?

Include your questions in the inquiry form or email us directly. We're happy to answer questions about our programs, philosophy, or availability before you schedule a visit.
