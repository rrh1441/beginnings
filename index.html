Programs by Age

Whether your child is 3 months or 3 years old, we have a classroom designed for exactly where they are right now.

Infants

3–12 months

1:4 teacher-to-child ratio

Your baby gets one-on-one attention from caregivers who learn their cues, their schedule, and what makes them smile. You get daily photos and peace of mind.

Learn More

Toddlers

1–3 years

1:5 teacher-to-child ratio

This is when language explodes and personalities emerge. We channel all that toddler energy into painting, building, outdoor play, and learning to be a good friend.

Learn More

Preschool

3–5 years

1:8 teacher-to-child ratio

By the time they leave for kindergarten, your child will write their name, count past 20, and—more importantly—know how to ask questions, solve problems, and work with others.

How Enrollment Works

1

Submit an Inquiry

Fill out our simple online form with your child's information and preferred location. We'll follow up within 1–2 business days.

2

Tour the School

Visit your preferred location to see our classrooms, meet teachers, and ask questions. Tours are typically 30–45 minutes.

3

Receive an Offer

When a spot opens for your child's age group, we'll extend an enrollment offer and share tuition and paperwork details.

4

Start & Orientation

Before your child's first day, we'll schedule an orientation visit and create a personalized transition plan together.
