Two Locations, Same Great Care

Whether you're in Queen Anne or Capitol Hill, you get the same experienced teachers, small classes, and approach that's kept Seattle families coming back for 30 years.

Queen Anne

123 Queen Anne Ave N, Seattle, WA 98109

Nestled in the heart of Lower Queen Anne, our original location offers a warm, home-like setting with easy access for families commuting through downtown Seattle. Founded in 1992, this center carries on our cooperative roots and strong community traditions.

Capitol Hill

456 15th Ave E, Seattle, WA 98112

Located in a vibrant Capitol Hill neighborhood, our second campus brings the same nurturing philosophy to families on the east side of the city. Opened in 2018, this modern facility features purpose-built spaces designed for early learning.
