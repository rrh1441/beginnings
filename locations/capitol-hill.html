About Our Capitol Hill Center

Located in a vibrant Capitol Hill neighborhood, our second campus brings the same nurturing philosophy to families on the east side of Seattle.

Opened in 2018, this modern facility was purpose-built for early learning with careful attention to natural light, flexible spaces, and child-scaled design. Our experienced teaching team includes educators who helped launch this location and bring deep expertise in infant development and inclusive practices.

The center sits just blocks from Volunteer Park, which we visit regularly for nature walks and outdoor exploration. The large outdoor playground features age-appropriate climbing structures, a water play area, and garden spaces.

What Makes Capitol Hill Special

  • Modern, purpose-built early learning space with natural light
  • Large outdoor playground with climbing structures and water play
  • Dedicated art studio and sensory room
  • Walking distance to Volunteer Park for nature excursions
  • State-of-the-art infant sleep and care spaces
  • Easy access from Madison Valley, Montlake, and Central District

A Typical Day

Each day balances structure and flexibility, with time for exploration, rest, outdoor play, and connection.

7:00–9:00 AM Arrival, breakfast, and free play with morning activities
9:00–11:30 AM Circle time, project work, and outdoor play
11:30–12:30 PM Family-style lunch and transition to rest
12:30–3:00 PM Nap and quiet time (varies by age group)
3:00–4:00 PM Afternoon snack and small-group activities
4:00–6:00 PM Outdoor play, free choice, and gradual pickup

Find Us

Parking: On-site parking lot for drop-off and pickup. Street parking also available on 15th Ave E. Accessible via Metro routes 10, 11, and 49.
